Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - 2/3 cups vegetable oil
 - 3/4 cups sugar
 - 1 1/2 cups flour
 - 5 apples
 - 1 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 spoon vinegar
 - 1/2 teaspoon cinammon
 - some butter
  
Directions:
1. In a large bowl add the egg, oil, sugar and flour in this sequence. Stir (?) until you have a homogenous mix. Add cinammon.

2. Mesh (?) the apples without peeling them. Put the soda and vinegar in them and stir.

3. Take a wide pan and put a thin layer of butter on it. Mix the apples with other ingredients and put them into the pan. Put some sugar over the cake.

4. Put the pan into preheat to 180 degrees C oven. Bake about 40 minutes.
